Our Plan

Objective: Compare the learning performance of evolving fuzzy X-Pilot agents trained with a Genetic Algorithm and with a Neural Network.

We are going to be evolving two fuzzy controllers in X-Pilot, one with neural networks and the other with genetic algorithms. They will be using the same production system to isolate the learning performance of the neural network versus the genetic algorithm.

We plan on having the two controllers compete individually against the built in robot Sel to evaluate the performance. In addition, we will have a competition between the neural network and the genetic algorithm for head-to-head results.
